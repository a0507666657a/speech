Basic Speech Therapy Exercises

These fundamental exercises can help improve speech clarity and articulation:

Lip Exercises

  • Pucker and stretch lips
  • Lip rounding exercises
  • Lip strength training

Tongue Exercises

  • Move tongue in different directions
  • Tongue tip elevation
  • Tongue lateralization

Breathing Exercises

  • Deep breathing techniques
  • Controlled exhalation
  • Diaphragmatic breathing

Vowel Sounds Practice

  • Long vowel sounds (a, e, i, o, u)
  • Short vowel sounds
  • Vowel combinations

Consonant Practice

  • Focus on difficult sounds
  • Consonant blends
  • Sound production techniques

Daily Practice Tips

  • Practice for 15-20 minutes daily
  • Record yourself to track progress
  • Start with easier exercises and gradually increase difficulty
  • Stay hydrated during practice sessions
  • Take breaks when needed
Important Note: These exercises are general guidelines. Always consult with a licensed speech therapist for personalized treatment plans and professional guidance.
